About Planova

Planova is a modern event discovery and ticketing platform designed to help people find club nights, concerts, festivals, and student events with ease. Our mission is to make nightlife and entertainment more accessible, transparent, and enjoyable for everyone.

We work closely with event organisers to ensure accurate listings, secure ticketing, and a seamless experience from browsing to checkout. Planova is built with a focus on speed, simplicity, and user trust.
